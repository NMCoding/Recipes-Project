Lasagna

Description

This no-bake skillet lasagna is made right on your stovetop and is a fast and easy alternative to store-bought hamburger mixes!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 jar spaghetti sauce
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 onion chopped
  • 1 jar clove garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 cups dried malfada noodles
  • 1 cup shredded mozzerella cheese

STEPS

  1. Step 1
  2. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook and stir beef in the hot skillet until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes. Drain and discard grease. Add spaghetti sauce, tomatoes, onion, garlic, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Cook over low heat until sauce is hot, about 15 minutes.
  3. Step 2
  4. Meanwhile, fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rolling boil. Cook mafalda noodles at a boil until tender yet firm to the bite, about 8 minutes. Drain.
  5. Step 3
  6. Add cooked and drained noodles to the sauce and stir until completely coated. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese on top.
  7. Step 4
  8. Set an oven rack about 6 inches from the heat source and preheat the oven's broiler.
  9. Step 5
  10. Place skillet under the hot broil and cook until cheese is golden and bubbly, 3 to 5 minutes.
